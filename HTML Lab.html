I found a rocket as well, but it flew off to fast for me to follow. The monsters down here don't like us either, so we have to be very careful and explore in the dark. Our boss put me in charge of defending our base since I haven't had much luck with exporing. So now I get to fly the plane around the base! They said it was punishment, but this has been a lot of fun!
